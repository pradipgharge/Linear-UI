List

Lists are continuous, vertical indexes of text or images. They are used to display the data or any information in web pages in the ordered or unordered form.

Variants available: list-style-disc, list-style-circle & list-style-square

Ordered List

Variants available: list-style-decimal, list-style-upper-roman, list-style-lower-roman, list-style-upper-alpha, list-style-lower-alpha.
Along with each of these classes you can add the reversed attribute to the <ol> tag for a reverse ordered list.
