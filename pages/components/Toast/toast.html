Toast

Toast is a small message that shows up in a box at the bottom of the screen and disappears on its own after few seconds.It only fills the amount of space required for the message and the current activity remains visible and interactive.

Simple Toast

Connection timed out. Showing limited messages.

Your photos have been archived.

                            
                                    <div class="toast toast-gray-bg">
                                        <p class="toast-title">Connection timed out. Showing limited messages.</p>
                                        <button class="btn-toast">RETRY</button>
                                    </div>
                                    <div class="toast toast-info">
                                        <p class="toast-title">Your photos have been archived.</p>
                                        <button class="btn-toast">UNDO</button>
                                    </div>
